Description

Our website has biodiversity statistics on 56 unique national parks situated in the United States of America.
What you can do with our website :

  • Search up Parks : This page (under the Parks tab) allows you to search up parks by their name or view parks by state. A map also highlights the states which contain the resulting National Parks
  • Search up Species : This search (under tab Species) will allow you to search for species in our database by a variety of search fields, a map is also displayed that highlights the states that have the searched for species. The search fields include :
    • Scientific/Common Name : Enter a full scientific/common name or even a fragment
    • Category : Choose from 14 categories that our species are grouped by
    • Order : Enter the order of the species you'd like to search for
    • Family : Enter the family of species you'd like to look up
    • Park : Select a park from which you'd like to see results for
    • State : Select state for which you'd like to view species of
